## Product Philosophy

### Core Beliefs

**1. AI augments product leaders and enginnering managers, never replaces them**
- AI Platform Leaders and enginnering managers make final decisions
- AI handles tedious extraction and analysis
- Human expertise + machine speed = better outcomes
- Confidence scores on everything

**2. Accuracy is non-negotiable**
- 95%+ accuracy or we don't ship
- Clear "AI Confidence Score" tags
- Clear "Signal Tier - ACT (act this week) or WATCH (track over 30 days)" tags
- Validated on thousands of AI Signals
- Transparency on model limitations

**3. Security is table stakes**
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- Zero-retention policy option

**4. Speed unlocks value**
- Dashboard should be super fast to use

## Product Overview

### Core Features

**Themes Dashboard**
- Dashboard showing Weekly Clusters/Themes
- Ability to filter themes by Signal Tier: ACT (act this week) or WATCH (track over 30 days)
- Also some metrics on top like - total signals processed, Active Clusters, ACT Signals, Confidence Average

**Confidence Scoring**
- Automatically tag confidence level for each signal

**Signal Classification based on trend velocity**

**Weekly Briefs on new identified Signals**

**Summary of each Signal**
- User can click on any of the signals to understand more - Why it matters, how does it impact Enterprise Platforms, citations etc..

**Citation of each source the signal is based on to gain user trust**

**Download Weekly Brief**
